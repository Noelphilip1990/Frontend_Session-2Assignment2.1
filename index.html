Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for a deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Methods

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if your making an omelette)
  5. When using theeggs are still moist remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate untill served)
